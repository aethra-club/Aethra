Getting Started with Aethra

1. Join the Community

Follow us on Instagram and LinkedIn to stay updated with the latest events and announcements.

2. Register for Events

Browse our events page and register for workshops, hackathons, and community meetups that interest you.

3. Network & Collaborate

Connect with fellow students, mentors, and industry professionals. Build your network and find teammates for hackathons.

4. Build & Learn

Participate in workshops to learn new skills, join hackathons to build projects, and share your knowledge with others.

Code of Conduct

01

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect and kindness. We welcome people of all backgrounds, skill levels, and experiences.

02

Be Inclusive

Foster an environment where everyone feels welcome to participate, learn, and contribute.

03

Be Collaborative

Share knowledge, help others, and work together. Learning is better when we support each other.

04

Be Honest

Maintain academic integrity. Submit original work and give credit where credit is due.

05

Be Constructive

Provide helpful feedback and accept feedback graciously. Focus on growth and improvement.

Hackathon Survival Guide

Before the Event

  • ✓ Register early and read the event rules
  • ✓ Form a team or be open to joining one
  • ✓ Brainstorm project ideas
  • ✓ Set up your development environment
  • ✓ Get familiar with APIs and tools you'll use

During the Event

  • ✓ Attend the opening ceremony
  • ✓ Start with a clear plan and MVP
  • ✓ Take breaks and stay hydrated
  • ✓ Ask mentors for help when stuck
  • ✓ Document your progress
  • ✓ Prepare a clear demo and presentation

Judging Criteria

  • ★ Innovation & Creativity
  • ★ Technical Implementation
  • ★ Design & User Experience
  • ★ Impact & Practicality
  • ★ Presentation Quality

After the Event

  • ✓ Submit your project on time
  • ✓ Share your experience on social media
  • ✓ Connect with teammates and new friends
  • ✓ Continue developing your project
  • ✓ Add it to your portfolio

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a student to participate?

Yes, Aethra events are primarily for students. However, recent graduates and young professionals are also welcome at most events.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Not at all! We welcome beginners and offer workshops specifically designed for those new to programming. Our hackathons have tracks for all skill levels.

How do I find a team for hackathons?

You can form a team with friends or use our team formation sessions during events. Many participants also find teammates through our community channels.

Are events free to attend?

Yes! All Aethra events are completely free for participants. We believe in making tech education accessible to everyone.

Can I participate remotely?

Most of our hackathons support both in-person and virtual participation. Check the specific event details for participation options.

How can I become a mentor or sponsor?

We'd love to have you! Please reach out through our contact page or email us at aethra.club@gmail.com.
