Our Mission

Students rarely get to face open-ended challenges, make decisions, fail, and rebuild in school settings.

Hackathons help bring that missing layer into schools. They turn theory into action, give students ownership of their ideas, and replace rote learning with experience.

Our mission is simple: to bring this kind of learning into schools around the world. Every school, every student, not just those with resources or elite programs, should be able to host hackathons.

About Us

Aethra is a student-run initiative that aims to bring Hackathons to secondary schools around the world. We partner with schools to host internal hackathons that are designed exclusively for students and managed completely by our team.

We understand that schools are always looking for new ways to engage students beyond the classroom. Organizing large-scale extracurricular events can be difficult to balance with academic priorities. Aethra makes it simple by handling every part of the hackathon process, including setup, registration, judging, and certificates.

Our Future Vision

01

Global Innovation Hubs

Over the next few years, Aethra will establish a global network of student-run innovation hubs within schools, where hackathons aren't one-time events but an integral part of how students learn.

02

Hackathons as Student Life

Our vision is to make hackathons a staple of student life, just like sports teams or MUN clubs, but focused on solving real problems with technology.

03

Building Tomorrow's Solutions

Imagine schools where students compete to design tools that make classrooms more inclusive, cities more sustainable, or healthcare more accessible. That's the future we're building.

04

Scale & Mentorship

Aethra's next chapter is about scale, experimenting with frameworks and formats, and mentorship programs that connect thousands of young builders worldwide.
